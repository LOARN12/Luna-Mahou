The Locking Charm


Locking charm is a magical spell used to secure doors or objects from being opened. It is often seen in fantasy stories, especially in the Harry Potter series. The spell creates a magical lock that cannot be bypassed by physical means. Only someone with the correct counter-spell or magical key can unlock it. This charm is useful for protecting valuable or dangerous items.
