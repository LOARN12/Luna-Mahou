Specialty: Dark Arts & Parseltongue
Legacy: Chamber of Secrets

The Ambitious Founder

Salazar Slytherin was one of the four founding wizards of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. A brilliant, ambitious, and powerful magician, he was renowned for his mastery of the dark arts and his controversial beliefs about magical bloodlines.

The Pure-Blood Philosophy

Salazar believed that only pure-blood witches and wizards should be allowed to study at Hogwarts. He feared that Muggle-borns might bring danger or weakness to the wizarding world. This belief eventually led to serious conflict with his fellow founders, especially Godric Gryffindor.

The Chamber of Secrets

About Slytherin House

Discover the depths of our ancient house

House Colors & Symbolism

Slytherin's colors are emerald green and silver, representing the house's connection to water, ambition, and nobility. The serpent, our emblematic animal, symbolizes wisdom, power, and transformation.

Common Room

Located beneath the Black Lake in the dungeons, our common room features an underwater view that creates an ethereal green glow. The room is adorned with dark wood, silver fixtures, and comfortable leather furniture.

House Ghost

The Bloody Baron serves as Slytherin's house ghost. Known for his stern demeanor and the chains he wears as penance, he maintains order among Slytherin students and commands respect throughout Hogwarts.

House Values

While often misunderstood, Slytherin values include self-preservation, loyalty to family and friends, and the pursuit of excellence. We believe in using all means necessary to achieve our goals.
