The reductor Charm

Reducto

The Reductor Curse is a powerful spell used to blast solid objects into pieces. It is cast using the incantation "Reducto!". This spell is often used in duels to destroy obstacles or threats. It can cause serious damage if used on living beings. Ginny Weasley famously mastered this curse in Dumbledore's Army.
