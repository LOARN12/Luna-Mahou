The Animation Charm


Piertotem Locomotor

Piertotum Locomotor is a spell used in the Harry Potter series to animate statues and suits of armor. Professor McGonagall casts it during the Battle of Hogwarts to defend the school. The spell brings stone figures to life, turning them into powerful protectors. It is a non-verbal charm but can also be cast aloud for dramatic effect. The spell shows the magical ability to command inanimate objects to fight or guard.
