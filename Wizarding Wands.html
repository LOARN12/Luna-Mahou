Harry Potter's Wand

Length: 11"

Wood: Holly

Core: Phoenix Feather

Lore: Loyal and powerful, it shares a twin core with Voldemort’s wand.

Fun Fact: Its core came from Fawkes, Dumbledore’s phoenix — who only gave two feathers.

Hermione Granger's Wand

Length: 10¾"

Wood: Vine

Core: Dragon Heartstring

Lore: Precise and intelligent, suited for a brilliant witch like Hermione.

Fun Fact: Vine wood is associated with seekers of knowledge — perfect for the brightest witch of her age.

Draco Malfoy's Wand

Length: 10"

Wood: Hawthorn

Core: Unicorn Hair

Lore: Proud and Complex, reflecting Draco’s inner conflicts and journey.

Fun Fact: It changed allegiance to Harry in Deathly Hallows, helping him master the Elder Wand.

Luna Lovegood's Wand

Wood: Unique

Core: Unknown

Lore: Dreamy, Free-spirited, and wonderfully unique — just like Luna herself.

Fun Fact: Her second wand was crafted by Ollivander during captivity — custom for Luna's unique magic.

Ron Weasley's Wand

Length: 14"

Wood: Willow

Core: Unicorn Hair

Lore: Loyal and Warm-hearted, this wand complements Ron’s dependable nature.

Fun Fact: Unicorn hair cores are loyal — just like Ron — but not great with ambitious magic.

Severus Snape's Wand

Details: Not officially known

Lore: Shrouded in secrecy, yet clearly powerful and precise in the hands of a master.

Fun Fact: Snape’s wand was never revealed in the books, but it’s rumored to be made of ebony with a dragon heartstring core.

Albus Dumbledore's Wand

Length: 15"

Wood: Elder

Core: Thestral Tail Hair

Lore: The fabled Elder Wand, one of the Deathly Hallows, wielded by the wise and powerful Dumbledore.

Fun Fact: The Elder Wand is unbeatable in duels, but its power comes with a heavy price — it must be won, not given.

Lord Voldemort's Wand

Length: 13½"

Wood: Yew

Core: Phoenix Feather

Lore: Ruthless and Dark, twin to Harry’s wand, symbolizing fate’s intertwined path.

Fun Fact: Voldemort’s wand was made from the same phoenix feather as Harry’s, but from Fawkes’ twin, symbolizing their eternal conflict.

Sirius Black's Wand

Details: Length and core not revealed

Lore: Rebellious and noble, Sirius’s wand suited his bold, untamed spirit.

Fun Fact:Though never described in detail, fan theories suggest it was highly expressive and powerful.

Bellatrix Lestrange's Wand

Length: 12¾"

Wood: Walnut

Core: Dragon Heartstring

Lore: Twisted and Fierce, Bellatrix’s wand echoes her madness and loyalty to the Dark Lord.

Fun Fact: Crafted from walnut with a dragon heartstring core, it was as unyielding and twisted as her soul.
