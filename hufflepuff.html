Hufflepuff House

"Those patient Hufflepuffs are true and unafraid of toil."

Founder: Helga Hufflepuff

Helga Hufflepuff was one of the four founders of Hogwarts. She was known for her kindness, fairness, and inclusive spirit. She took in students whom the other founders might have overlooked and treated everyone equally.

History of Hufflepuff

Founded over a thousand years ago, Hufflepuff House values hard work, loyalty, patience, and fair play. The house animal is the badger, symbolizing tenacity and protection. Hufflepuff students are known for their dedication and kindness to all.

House Traits

  • Hard Work
  • Loyalty
  • Patience
  • Fair Play
  • Inclusiveness
  • Dedication
